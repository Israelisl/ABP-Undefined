About AgriRS Lab

The Agricultural Remote Sensing Laboratory (AgriRS Lab) at INPE is dedicated to advancing the science and applications of remote sensing in agriculture.

Our mission is to develop innovative methods for monitoring agricultural systems using Earth observation data, contributing to food security and sustainable land use.

Learn more about our research

Research Areas

We focus on cutting-edge research in agricultural remote sensing with applications in precision farming, crop monitoring, and environmental sustainability.

Crop Monitoring

Developing algorithms for crop classification, yield prediction, and growth monitoring using satellite imagery.

Water Resources

Assessing water use efficiency and irrigation management through remote sensing techniques.

Climate Change

Studying the impacts of climate variability on agricultural systems using Earth observation data.
